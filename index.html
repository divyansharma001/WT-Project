Features

Built for how you actually feel.

Everything haven offers is designed around one idea: meeting you where you are, without judgment, without friction.

Mood-aware matching

Tell haven how you feel and we'll suggest the right mentor for this moment — calm for anxious days, energetic for stuck ones.

Eight distinct mentors

From compassionate listeners to coping strategists, each mentor has a unique voice so you can choose who you want to talk to.

Evidence-based tools

Gentle CBT reframing, grounding exercises, breathwork, and self-compassion practices — woven naturally into conversation.

Private by design

Conversations stay in your browser session. No accounts, no database, no tracking. Your API key lives only on your device.

Crisis-aware

haven recognizes moments that need more than a chat and surfaces 988 Lifeline and Crisis Text Line resources immediately.

Export when you want

Download any conversation as a transcript so you can revisit reflections, share with a therapist, or keep a journal of your own.

Privacy

Your words. Your device. That's it.

haven runs entirely in your browser. There's no account, no analytics, no server storing your chats. When you close the tab, everything closes with it.

  • No server. Nothing you say leaves your browser, except the direct call to your AI provider.
  • No accounts. No email, no password, no profile to hack.
  • Your key, your rules. Bring your own Gemini key — stored locally, never transmitted to us.
  • Session-only memory. Close the tab and the conversation is gone. That simple.
